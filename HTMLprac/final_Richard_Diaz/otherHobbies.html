My Hobbies

Cardistry

Starting a few months ago I got into a hobby known as Cardistry or also known as Card Flourishing. "Card flourishes are visual displays of skill performed with playing cards, designed to show the skill or manual dexterity of a 'flourisher'. Card flourishes are primarily intended to be visually impressive and to appear difficult to do."[1]

Want to see or learn more about Cardistry? Have a quick look at YouTube.

Cycling

A few years back after my family split apart and I moved, I bought my self a bike. I haven't ridden a bike since I was a kid, but I wanted one to start commuting to school and to work as a means to save gas. But once I started, I loved it. "Cycling is widely regarded as a very effective and efficient mode of transportation optimal for short to moderate distances."[2] But Cycling became another part of me that has developed into more than just commuting. The feeling of going fast through my own power made me feel enlightened and I started entering into races around the county.
